# Sales KPI Dashboard (Excel)

A dynamic Microsoft Excel dashboard developed to transform raw sales transactions into executive-level business insights through automated calculations, interactive KPI cards, and structured reporting. The workbook demonstrates how Excel can function as a lightweight business intelligence platform by combining structured data tables, advanced formulas, and dashboard design principles to deliver real-time sales performance monitoring.

The project emphasizes automation, clarity, and usability without relying on macros or VBA, making it suitable for business users and analysts alike.

## Workbook Structure

The workbook is organized into three primary worksheets.

### Raw_Data

Stores all sales transactions, including:

* Order Date
* Product
* Region
* Revenue
* Profit
* Sales Information

### KPI_Calculations

Processes business metrics through formulas including:

* Total Revenue
* Total Profit
* Best Performing Region
* Top Product
* Revenue Rankings
* Supporting Calculations

### Dashboard

Provides an executive summary featuring:

* KPI Cards
* Revenue Overview
* Profit Summary
* Regional Performance
* Product Performance
* Interactive Business Metrics

## How to Use

1. Open the Excel workbook.
2. Navigate to the **Raw_Data** worksheet.
3. Enter or update sales transactions.
4. Allow the formulas to recalculate automatically.
5. Open the **Dashboard** worksheet to view updated KPIs and business metrics.

## Business Value

Organizations often rely on spreadsheets for sales reporting, but manual calculations can be time-consuming and prone to errors. This dashboard automates the aggregation and analysis of sales data using native Excel functionality, providing management with an interactive and continuously updated view of key business metrics. By combining structured tables, dynamic formulas, and intuitive dashboard design, the solution improves reporting accuracy and enables faster, data-driven decision-making.
